Hi.

Just a random high schooler from the metropolis that is Shanghai.

So… Nice to meet you, stranger. Let’s begin.

Me

So umm… Since this blog is about me, I might as well introduce myself.

I’m a high schooler from Shanghai, currently studying at Shanghai High School. The scenery is great here. We don’t allow smartphones, computers and stuff like that, though. So we can concentrate on our studies.

It’s a nice place overall.

Stuff I Like

Cars and planes. In particular, fast cars and passenger planes.
Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula 1 team is the best.
The Airbus A350 is a piece of artwork. Simply beautiful.

Games as well. GTA5 and racing sims like the F1 series and Forza. Really want an Assetto Corsa or some other hardcore sim. Been wanting a flight sim as well, but no XSX…so that’ll have to wait a bit. (Sorry, Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020!)

Oh, right. DDLC as well. Who’d have thought?
Sayori’s the best. Just Sayori.

Books are cool as well. John Green is a fabulous writer. The different interpretations of life in Paper Towns opens up a significant chunk of my thoughts.

DJing also seems nice. I’d kinda like a DJ machine, though much will need to be learned before I can do some mediocre DJing.

This seems like a stream of consciousness. Or not.

Me In the Future

What will I be like in…10 years? 20 years?
My dream job is probably a racing driver or pilot, but everybody says you gotta learn medicine or economics so you can get a good job and earn money. Guess it’s pretty hard to pursue your own dreams nowadays.
Although money is a really effective incentive…

But sometimes, getting rich doesn’t seem as important as living life to its happiest and fullest.

Still, people change, and so do their thoughts. Maybe I’ll be happy with simply money 20 years from now. Pressure mounts as you grow up. You don’t have much time to think about deep stuff.

Yeah. But I still hope money won’t be the only thing on my mind.

That’s it I guess. You probably know a bit about me now, and maybe you like me, or maybe you don’t.
It’s not a big deal, though. People change all the time. Live life to its happiest for now. That’s what I keep trying to tell myself.

Thanks for reading this, stranger. Wish you all the best.
